# Bhopal Air Quality Monitor (AQI Simulator)

## Overview
This project is a Python-based Air Quality Index (AQI) monitoring system that simulates air quality conditions in Bhopal based on seasonal patterns.

It generates an AQI reading and provides:
- Air quality category
- Health warnings
- Recommended precautions

The objective is to help users understand air pollution levels and take appropriate safety measures.

---

## Problem Statement
Air pollution is a significant concern in many Indian cities, including Bhopal. However, people often lack clear information about:
- The meaning of AQI values
- The impact of air quality on health
- Necessary precautions at different pollution levels

This project addresses the problem by simulating AQI data and presenting it in a simple and structured format.

---

## Features
- Simulates AQI based on seasonal trends (Winter, Summer, Monsoon, Post-Monsoon)
- Categorizes air quality levels (Good to Severe)
- Displays health-related warnings
- Provides practical precautions for each AQI category
- Clean and structured console output
